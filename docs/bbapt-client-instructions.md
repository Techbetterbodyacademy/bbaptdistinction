# Getting Started with Better Body Academy

Welcome aboard. This is your guide to using the Better Body Academy app. Takes about 5 minutes to read end-to-end. Bookmark this page if you want to refer back.

The app lives at **https://bbapt.vercel.app**. Works on your phone or computer. No download needed.

---

## First Time Logging In

Your coach has invited you. Here's how you get in.

**Step 1.** Check your email inbox for a message titled something like "Your coach just invited you to coaching." It looks like this:

> **MY WORKSPACE**
>
> **[Coach name], you are in.**
>
> [Coach name] invited you to start coaching. Click the button below to sign in and fill out a short intake. Takes about 5 minutes.
>
> **Start your intake →**

**Step 2.** Click the "Start your intake" button.

**Step 3.** You'll land on the intake form. Fill out:
- Why now (what's making you start)
- Past attempts (what you've tried before)
- Current constraints (injuries, time limits, schedule)
- Primary goal (lose weight, build muscle, get healthier, etc.)
- Realistic timeframe (when do you want results by)
- Health flags (any conditions your coach needs to know about)

Be honest. Your coach needs this to build the right program for you. Takes about 5 minutes.

**Step 4.** Click Submit. You're now in.

If the email doesn't arrive in your inbox, check your spam folder. If it's not there, message your coach and they'll send you the link directly via WhatsApp or SMS.

## Logging In Going Forward

After your first login you have two ways to get in:

**Option A: Sign in with Google** (recommended if your email is a Gmail address)
1. Go to https://bbapt.vercel.app
2. Click **Continue with Google**
3. Pick your Google account
4. Check your email for a 6-digit code
5. Enter the code and click Verify
6. You're in

**Option B: Email and password**
1. Go to https://bbapt.vercel.app
2. Type your email and password
3. Click Sign in
4. You're in

**Forgot your password?** Click the "Forgot password?" link on the sign-in page. You'll get a 6-digit code by email. Enter it, then set a new password.

**Heads up:** if you leave the tab open and idle for 30 minutes, the app signs you out automatically for security. You'll see a warning 2 minutes before it happens with a "Stay signed in" button.

## Your Home Screen

After login you land on your home dashboard. Here's what you'll see:

- **Hey, [your first name].** Your greeting at the top.
- **Three stat cards** showing:
  - **Status** (are you Active, Paused, or Completed)
  - **Start weight** (what you weighed when you started)
  - **Coach** (your coach's name)
- **What happens next** — what's on your plate today or this week

At the top of the screen there's a row of tabs. From left to right:

**Home · Program · Habits · Sessions · Check-ins · Logbook · Library · Meal plan · Messages**

We'll go through each one below.

## The Tabs Explained

### Program
**What it shows:** the training program your coach built for you. Listed week by week, day by day. Each day shows your workout name (e.g., "Upper body push").

**What to do:** click today's workout to start logging it (see "How to Log a Workout" below).

### Sessions
**What it shows:** the list of every workout you've completed, newest first. Click any session to see what you did (exercises, sets, reps, weights, notes).

**What to do:** review your last few sessions before the next one to know where you left off.

### Habits
**What it shows:** the habits your coach assigned (e.g., 10,000 steps, 7 hours sleep, no alcohol on weeknights).

**What to do:** each day, tap the habits you completed. Your coach sees how consistent you've been.

### Check-ins
**What it shows:** your past weekly check-ins, and a button to start a new one.

**What to do:** once a week (your coach will tell you which day), click "New check-in" and submit your photos and numbers. See "How to Do a Weekly Check-in" below.

### Logbook
**What it shows:** a personal journal you can use however helps you (food diary, mood notes, training thoughts).

**What to do:** optional. Some clients write daily, some never use it. Up to you.

### Library
**What it shows:** resources your coach has shared (PDFs, videos, articles). Filtered to what's relevant to you.

**What to do:** read or watch whatever your coach pointed you at. Reference it anytime.

### Meal plan
**What it shows:** the personalized 7-day meal plan your coach generated for you. Includes a note from your coach, all your meals with macros and ingredients, and a shopping list at the bottom.

**What to do:** review the plan, do the shopping list before each week, follow the meals. If you have older plans, switch between them using the "Older plans" dropdown.

### Messages
**What it shows:** your private chat with your coach.

**What to do:** anything. Ask questions, share wins, flag struggles. Your coach replies within 24 hours.

## How to Log a Workout (Step by Step)

This is the main thing you'll do day to day. Pull up the app right before or during your workout.

**Step 1.** Click **Program** in the top nav.

**Step 2.** Find today's workout on your program. It's usually labeled by day (e.g., "Week 3 · Day 2"). Tap it.

**Step 3.** You'll see the list of exercises for today.

**Step 4.** For each exercise:
- Tap **Add set**
- Enter:
  - **Reps** (how many you did)
  - **Weight** (kg or lbs)
  - **RPE** (Rate of Perceived Exertion, 1 to 10. 1 = easy, 10 = couldn't have done another rep)
- Tap Save
- Repeat for each set

**Step 5.** Add notes if anything was off (e.g., "right shoulder felt tight today" or "best set ever").

**Step 6.** When the workout's done, tap **Finish workout**.

The session is now in your Sessions tab. Your coach sees it on their side and can comment.

## How to Do a Weekly Check-in (Step by Step)

Your coach will tell you what day each week to do this. Usually Sunday.

**Step 1.** Click **Check-ins** in the top nav.

**Step 2.** Click **New check-in**.

**Step 3.** **Upload your photos.** Most coaches want three:
- Front (whole body, neutral pose)
- Side (whole body, side profile)
- Back (whole body)

Stand in the same spot, same lighting, same time of day, same clothing each week. Consistency is what makes the photos useful for tracking.

**Step 4.** **Enter your weight** in kg.

**Step 5.** Rate the following on a 1-10 scale:
- **Sleep** (how well did you sleep this week)
- **Energy** (how energetic did you feel)
- **Adherence** (how closely did you follow the plan: workouts, habits, meals)

**Step 6.** **What went well this week?** Write 2-3 sentences. What did you nail? Where did you feel proud?

**Step 7.** **What was a struggle?** Write 2-3 sentences. What got in the way? What pissed you off? Be honest. This is where your coach can actually help.

**Step 8.** Tap **Submit**.

Your coach gets notified, reviews everything, and responds via Messages within 24 hours.

## How to Read Your Meal Plan

**Step 1.** Click **Meal plan** in the top nav.

**Step 2.** Read the **coach note** at the top. It's a short message from your coach about what to prioritize this week.

**Step 3.** Scroll through the 7 days. Each day shows:
- Total calories and protein/carbs/fat for the day
- Each meal with:
  - Name
  - Macros per meal
  - Ingredient list with portions (e.g., "150g chicken breast", "1 cup brown rice")

**Step 4.** Scroll to the bottom and open the **Shopping list**. Grouped by category (Produce, Proteins, Grains and Carbs, Dairy and Eggs, Pantry, Other). Do this shop once a week, on the same day each week, so the food's always in the house when you need it.

**Step 5.** Follow the meals. If something doesn't suit (allergy, dislike, supply issue), message your coach and they'll regenerate.

If you have older plans, you can scroll back through them using the **Older plans** dropdown at the top.

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't log in"
- Try the **Forgot password?** link on the sign-in page
- If that doesn't work, message your coach. They can resend your sign-in link.

### "The invite link gave me an error"
- Magic links expire if not used within an hour. Ask your coach to resend.
- Or open the link in a private/incognito browser window (sometimes another browser cache messes with it).

### "I logged a workout but it didn't save"
- Make sure you tapped **Finish workout** at the end (not just closed the tab)
- Check your Sessions list to confirm it's there
- If it's missing, message your coach with the date and they'll restore it

### "I can't upload my check-in photos"
- Make sure the photo file is under 10MB
- Try a different photo format (JPG works best, HEIC sometimes fails on older phones)
- If it keeps failing, send the photos directly to your coach via Messages

### "I don't see my meal plan"
- Your coach hasn't generated one yet. Message them.

### "The app signed me out"
- That's normal after 30 minutes of inactivity. Just sign back in.

### "Something else is broken"
- Message your coach. Describe what happened and they'll get it sorted.

## Quick Tips

- **Bookmark the app on your phone.** On iPhone, open Safari → tap Share → "Add to Home Screen." On Android, open Chrome → menu → "Add to home screen." It'll feel like a native app.
- **Log your workouts as you do them**, not after. Easier to remember and more accurate.
- **Do your check-in the same day each week.** Sunday evening works for most people.
- **Take your photos in the same lighting every time.** Morning, kitchen window, same outfit. Consistency = data your coach can use.
- **Don't ghost your coach.** If you're struggling, message them. That's literally their job.
